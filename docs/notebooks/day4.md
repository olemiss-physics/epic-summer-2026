## Presentation prep

After the talks, use the remaining time to finalize your plots and plan your 5-minute presentation for Friday.

**Your presentation should include:**

1. What physics question were you investigating?
2. What data did you use and how did you analyze it?
3. Show your key plot(s) — walk us through what you see
4. What does your result mean physically?
5. One thing you'd do differently or explore next

!!! tip "Keep it simple"
    You're presenting to a mixed audience — some know physics, some don't. Aim to explain your result so that a curious non-scientist could follow along. That's a skill professional physicists practice their whole careers.
