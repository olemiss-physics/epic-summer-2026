## Housing

You can participate as a **residential** or **commuter** student.

**Residential students** will be housed in South Residence Hall on campus. The program fee covers housing — you'll receive check-in details by email closer to the program start date.

**Commuter students** should plan to arrive by 8:20 AM each morning. Parking information will be sent with your confirmation email.

## What to bring

=== "Everyone"

    - A **positive attitude** — no experience needed, we'll teach you everything
    - **Questions** — there are no silly questions in science
    - Comfortable clothes (the building is air-conditioned, so a light layer is useful)
    - Sunscreen and water bottle for outdoor activities

=== "Commuter students"

    - Lunch (or money to buy lunch on campus — the dining halls will be open)
    - Any snacks you like for breaks

=== "Residential students"

    - Bedding or sleeping bag (twin XL bed in residence hall)
    - Toiletries and personal items
    - Spending money for any campus meals or activities in the evenings

!!! info "Laptops and devices"
    **You do not need to bring your own laptop.** We will provide Chromebooks for the computing sessions. If you want to bring your own device, that's fine too — just make sure it has a browser (Chrome works best).
